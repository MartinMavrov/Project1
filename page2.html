...And now a little bit about ME.

In April 2011, I finished my grad school in Bulgaria, major International Economic Relationships. After I graduated I wasn't sure what to do, like many of the kids who just got off college.

So I started looking for a job... any type of a job that will suit my degree. I was ready to accept any offer, no matter how reasonable it was. Anyhow after a year of searching for a career path I decided to start another degree and come to US.

After couple of months going back and forth with the agency which was organizing the whole trip and the job fair event I got a job offer from Alaska. The interview for the work visa also went well and I was finnaly ready to pack and start a new live far from home.

I grew up with western culture: movies , music etc. Even NBA was something that I followed very close back in the days,so going to US was a dream came true.

On May 18th 2012 I landed in Alaska. I spend the whole summer there working as a prep-cook. I had two jobs at the time and that gave me a chance to save some money and move to my next destibation - Brooklyn, Ny.

I moved to NY and thanks to couple of friends that I have here I was able to settle fairly well. I started working as a busboy in the end of 2012. After couple of months I found a job in Soho,Ny . I worked there for 2.5 years.Then slowly I become a waiter and in March 2016 I got an offer to manage the restaurant that I worked in as a waiter. I accept the offer and I worked there till September 2019.
